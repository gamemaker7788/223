An emergency during physics class

Time:December 18th,2025

Writer:王文钰

Helper:付梓妤

As of now, we still don't know the final result. But I believe this matter will have a satisfying result.

On Tuesday, we saw the football matches, and footballers in Class 223 won easily.

On Wednesday aftermoon physics teacher don't wanted us to seethe footiball matches.However, the second lesson that day afternoon is PE, but we heard bad information when we got to the playground. This information shocked our women's football team results had been cancelledy! The news shocked us deeply, and even some students planned to launch "The December 8th Uprising". Lately, the reporter interviewed the male and female athletes of Class 223. Hou Qinren, who led us to win in the difficult situation, and this victory had a lot to do with him. Ren Keyu, the football star of Class 223, said: "At the end of the penalty shootout, Mr. Hou thought Class 223 had won. Then, Liu Qingxi said, 'How was the judgment made?' However, Mr. Hou misheard it as 'What are you looking at?' She said angrily, 'How can your quality be so poor?' and then cancelled the results."

When we all thought there was no hope, the huge grey ex-soldier came, and it was our class teacher Mr. Li. He tried every means to please Mrs. Hou, and said all kinds of nice things. Finally, Mrs. Hou decided that we would have a rematch. What a great class teacher! He also made a great contribution to our winning the double championship.

On Thursday, Liu Shuhan, one of the female football team members of Class 223, said: "We don't need to have a rematch because Liu Qingzi took the initiative to apologize to Mrs. Hou. Duan Yankai has been directly adjusted to the first place!"

All the students in Class 22 were very happy because this meant that Class 22 got two championships again! I hope that Class 22 is sure to become better in the future!!

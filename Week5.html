Changes in the Situation

Time: March 19th, 2026

Writer: Wang Wenyu

Helper: Fu Ziyu

——————————

On March 18th, 2026, a great reform took place in Class 223 of Guiyang No.1 Middle School. At this important point in the second term of Grade 8, Zhang and Fu, two students did homework of other subjects openly during class. And this situation continued for quite a long time. And they caused the nearby students to start imitating. In the third class in the afternoon that day, math class, the class teacher suddenly shouted insults for the students near the door and emphasized these students didn't have to listen in math class from then on. On the second day morning, the class teacher still kept making sarcastic remarks.

Great Reform of Class 223!

Time: March 19th, 2026

Writer: Wang Wenyu

Helper: Fu Ziyu

——————————

On the second day afternoon,the great reform began, and it divided into four aspects:

Politics:

The class teacher said, "If you don't make contributions, we won't consider you either!"

economy:

The class teacher forced several selected students to bring plants. The reason was based on the sentence he said above.

Ideology:

The class teacher and the English teacher talked taking turns speaking. And what impressed me most was Ms Zhang said, "among morality, intelligence, physical education, aesthetics and labor,morality comes first followed by intelligence."

territory:

The class teacher made a rearrangement of the class seats. Although it rationalized the seating arrangement more reasonable, it doesn't mean much now that we will soon be able to choose our own seats.

The reform was to strengthen his position. However ,its positive impact on the class was huge, it not only effectively stopped the internal rat race in the class, but also maintained the dignity of the teachers.

- The end -

局势变化

时间：2026年3月19日

编剧：王文钅玉

助手：付木辛女予

——————————

2026年3月18日， 古陶一中223班进行了重大改革。在八年级第二学期的重要时刻，张和付，两名学生在课堂上公开做其他科目的作业。 这种情况持续了相当长一段时间。 他们让附近的学生开始模仿。 当天下午第三节课，数学课上，班主任突然在门口对学生大喊侮辱，并强调这些 从那以后，学生们在 数学课上就不用再听了。 第二天早上，班主任依然不断说讽刺的话。

223班大改革！

时间：2026年3月19日

编剧：王文钅玉

助手：付木辛女予

——————————

第二天下午，大改革开始，分为四个方面：

政治：

班主任说："如果你不捐款，我们也不会考虑你！"

经济：

班主任强迫几位被选中的学生带上植物。 原因基于他上面说的那句话。

意识形态：

班主任和 英语老师 轮流说话。 最让我印象深刻的是张女士说："在道德、智力、体育、美学和劳动中，道德 排第一，其次是智力。"

领地：

班主任重新安排了教室座位。虽然它让 座位安排更合理 ，但现在我们很快就能自己选座位，这也没什么意义。

改革旨在巩固他的地位。然而，它对该级别的积极影响巨大，不仅有效终止了内部的竞争，也维护了该级别老师们的尊严 。

- 结束 -

New Year's Day: A New Beginning, Everything Looks Fresh and Bright

Time:December 31th, 2025

Writer:Wang Qizhao

Helper:AI

New Year's Day, the first day of the Gregorian calendar year, marks the beginning of a brand new year. People all over the world celebrate the harvest of the past year on this day and welcome the future with hope.

In China, although New Year's Day is a statutory holiday, traditionally the Lunar New Year (Spring Festival) is the more valued "New Year." Nevertheless, modern people still celebrate New Year's Day in various ways: having meals with family and friends, watching New Year's Eve galas, making New Year's resolutions, or sending blessings on social media. Cities often hold light shows or countdown events, filled with a joyful atmosphere.

For many people, especially students, New Year's Day also means a short holiday, a good opportunity to relax and recharge after busy study or work.
